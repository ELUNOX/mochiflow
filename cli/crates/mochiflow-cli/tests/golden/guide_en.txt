MochiFlow — how to drive it

Four verbs — say it naturally to your AI, or use the explicit command:
  • discuss — talk through the why/what   (mochiflow-discuss)
  • plan    — turn it into a spec         (mochiflow-plan)
  • build   — implement the approved spec (mochiflow-build)
  • ship    — verify, then open the PR    (mochiflow-ship)

Two approvals are yours: approve the spec before build, and the PR before it is created.
